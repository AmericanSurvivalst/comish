Your First 90 Days in a New Sales Role

By Scottâ€¢7 min read

The first 90 days determine whether you succeed or struggle. Here is your week-by-week playbook.

Days 1-30: Learn

  • Master the product demo (record yourself, get feedback)
  • Shadow top performers on calls
  • Learn the CRM and sales tools
  • Understand your comp plan inside and out
  • Map your territory and identify top accounts

Days 31-60: Build

  • Start outbound prospecting
  • Take your first solo calls
  • Build relationships with SEs and support
  • Create your first pipeline
  • Get feedback on early deals

Days 61-90: Execute

  • Run your own full sales cycles
  • Close first deals (even small ones)
  • Refine your messaging based on what works
  • Build consistent daily habits

ðŸ’¡ Pro tip

In month one, focus on learning, not closing. Reps who rush to sell before understanding the product create bad habits.

Bottom Line

Month 1 is for learning, month 2 is for building, month 3 is for executing. Do not skip steps.
