Everyone says you need 3x pipeline coverage. But that number is made up. Here is how to figure out what you actually need.

Why 3x Is Wrong

The 3x rule assumes a 33% win rate. But win rates vary wildly by segment, product, and sales cycle. A rep with 50% win rate needs 2x. A rep with 20% win rate needs 5x.

  • $500K quota with 50% win rate = $1M pipeline needed (2x)

Factor In Sales Cycle

If your sales cycle is 90 days, you need that coverage 90 days before quarter end. Pipeline created in month 3 will not close in time.
