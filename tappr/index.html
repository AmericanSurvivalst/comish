FAQ

Questions?

How does Tappr work?

Your Tappr card has an NFC chip. Tap it against any smartphone and your profile opens instantly in their browser. No app needed.

Do I need an app?

Nope. Neither do the people you share with. Your profile opens directly in their browser.

What if I change jobs?

Update your profile at app.tappr.one. Changes appear instantly on all your cards. No reprinting needed.

Which phones work with NFC?

All iPhones from XR/XS and newer. Most Android phones from the last 5 years. About 90% of smartphones.

What's the subscription?

$3/month or $25/year. Includes a free PVC card, unlimited links, analytics, and lead capture. Cards also work forever with basic features without subscription.
